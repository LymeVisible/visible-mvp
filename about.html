About VISIBLE

Problem

Tick-borne diseases and chronic infectious syndromes often present with overlapping, multi-system symptoms that are difficult to interpret in standard clinical workflows.

Solution

VISIBLE is an explainable decision-support prototype that organizes symptoms into clinically meaningful patterns using open data principles and rule-based AI logic.

Coinfection Framework

  • Lyme disease (Borrelia burgdorferi)
  • Babesiosis
  • Anaplasmosis / Ehrlichiosis
  • Rickettsial infections
  • Bartonella-associated syndromes (emerging evidence)
  • Tick-borne viral illnesses (regional)

Purpose

Designed for early-stage clinical decision support and hypothesis generation — not diagnosis.
